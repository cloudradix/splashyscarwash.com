More than just a splash...

We'll clean your car properly whether you choose the Basic Splash, Xtreme or a wash in between.

At Splashy's Car Wash, we want to be your car wash. We've over engineered our facility to ensure we're able to take the widest variety of cars, trucks, sport utilities, vans and crossover vehicles.

Larger truck tires? We've got you covered! We can handle up to 15" wide tires, most car washes can't do the larger truck tires! Just stay within our maximum height restriction of 7'2".

Our Team
